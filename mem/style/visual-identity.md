**Vokabi visual identity (REDESIGNED 2026-05, replaces old glassmorphism):**
- Style: Duolingo-inspired "brutal playful" — solid colors, hard offset shadows, bold rounded shapes
- Primary: Bright green hsl(88 78% 45%)
- Secondary: Sky blue hsl(199 89% 53%)
- Accent: Yellow hsl(45 100% 55%)
- Fonts: Baloo 2 (headings, weight 800), Nunito (body, weight 600)
- Shadows: HARD solid offset shadows (0 4px 0 0 color), NOT soft blurry shadows
- Buttons: rounded-2xl, border-2, uppercase tracking-wide, active:translate-y-1 press effect
- NO glassmorphism, NO gradients, NO mesh/radial backgrounds — solid flat fills only
- Border-radius: 1rem default, rounded-3xl on cards
- WHY: Investors said previous design "looked like a Lovable template" — avoid generic AI-app glass/gradient tropes.
